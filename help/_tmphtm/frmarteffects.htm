Artistic Effects
 Previous  Next


The Artistic Effects feature of Photo Mud provides several artistic effects you can apply to Photos:
  • Balls
  • Bricks
  • Clouds
  • Colored Pencil
  • Gas Plasma
  • Glass Cells
  • Impressionist
  • Lens Flare
  • Pixelate
  • Pointillist
  • Polar Transformation
  • Puzzle Pieces
  • Rev Effect
  • Ripples
  • Segment by Color
  • Shadow
  • Skew
  • Sphere Warp
  • Swirl
  • Warp
  • Water Reflections
  • Wave Effect
  • Wind Effect
  • Zoom Wave

You can zoom in and out on the original and modified photos with the + and - buttons and with the mouse wheel. It may be useful to zoom in on the image to get a better idea of the changes being made. You can pan or move the photos by dragging the mouse on either photo.

Artistic Effects is under the Image menu of Photo Mud Editor.
