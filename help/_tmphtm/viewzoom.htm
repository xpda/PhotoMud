Zooming In and Out        
Previous  Next


You can zoom a photo in and out in Photo Mud Editor using the mouse wheel and the + and - keys. The = key also works in place of the + key. The Zoom commands under the View menu and the toolbars can also be used.

The Zoom Window command lets you drag a box around the part of the photo you want to view. Fit to Window sizes the photo so that it fills the editing window. You can select 25%, 50%, 100%, and 200% zoom from the View menu.

In Photo Mud Editor and Explorer, you can Press Ctrl-M to view the photo in full screen mode, or use the Full Screen command under the View menu. Press any key to return to the normal mode. While in the full screen mode, you can press Page Up or Page Down to browse through the photos in the current folder.
