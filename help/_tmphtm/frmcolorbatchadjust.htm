Batch Color Adjustment
 Previous  Next


The Color Adjustment option of the Convert Photos function in Photo Mud Explorer can be used to adjust colors on the files being converted. You can specify the brightness, contrast, and saturation settings for the photos, and red, green, and blue intensities. This makes it easy to modify a group of photos that are too dark, too red, etc.

You can click the arrow buttons to browse through the selected photos and preview the color changes.

You can zoom in and out on the original and modified photos with the + and - buttons and with the mouse wheel. You can pan or move the photos by dragging the mouse on either photo.
