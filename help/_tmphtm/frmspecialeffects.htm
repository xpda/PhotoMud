Special Effects
 Previous  Next


The Special Effects feature of Photo Mud provides several special effects you can apply to Photos:
  • Emboss -- Shows and embossed effect on the image. You can specify the embossing angle and depth.
  • Posterize -- Posterize limits the image to a small number of color levels. You can select 2 to 64 color levels.
  • Mosaic -- Mosaic makes the image look like it's made of giant pixels. You can specify the cell size.
  • Solarize -- Solarize mimics the accidental exposure of film to sunlight. It inverts the color intensities larger than the specified threshold to produce an artistic effect.
  • Intensity Detect -- Intensity detect sets each pixel color to 0 or 255, depending on whether it is above or below the specified threshold. This may be useful for artistic effects as well as converting a photo to black and white (not gray scale).
  • Add Random Noise

You can zoom in and out on the original and modified photos with the + and - buttons and with the mouse wheel. It may be useful to zoom in on the image to get a better idea of the changes being made. You can pan or move the photos by dragging the mouse on either photo.

Special Effects is under the Image menu of Photo Mud Editor.
