Undo and Redo    
Previous  Next


You can undo editing operations in Photo Mud Editor using the Ctrl-Z shortcut or using the Undo command under the Edit menu. You can redo, or undo an undo, with Ctrl-Y or with the Redo commend under the Edit menu. There are also Undo and Redo buttons on the tool bar.

The multilevel Undo requires a fair amount of disk space. On extremely large images, 100 megapixels or more (depending on the computer configuration), there may be insufficient memory to use multilevel Undo and Redo. If necessary, you can disable multilevel Undo in the Photo Mud Editing Options.
