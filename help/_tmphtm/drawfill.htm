Filling an Area of Similar Color  
Previous  Next


You can fill an area of similar color using the Fill Area of Similar Color command under the Draw menu or toolbar, and clicking the mouse on color in the photo you want to fill. That part of the photo with a color similar to the color you selected will be filled with the current drawing color.

The Color Tolerance is the range of color intensities to be included in the Fill and Select Similar Color commands. The larger the number, the wider the selection in the image.

There is an upper and a lower tolerance. The upper tolerance specifies the range of colors to be selected that are brighter than the color at the designated pixel. The lower tolerance specifies the range of colors to be selected that are darker than the color at the designated pixel.

The designated pixel is the point in the photo that was clicked on.
