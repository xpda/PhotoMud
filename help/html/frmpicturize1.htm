Photo Mosaic

The Photo Mosaic feature of Photo Mud Editor allows you to create an image made up of many small "cell" images.

Here's how to make a photo mosaic:

  1. Copy the "cell" images into a single folder.

  2. Open the image you want to convert to a photo mosaic.

  3. Select the Photo Mosaic command from the Tools menu and enter the folder containing the cell images.

  4. Enter the options.

  5. Complete the operation and save the result. Be careful not to overwrite your original photo.

For better results, use a large number of cell images with a variety of dominant colors.
