Slideshow

To make a slideshow of various images, tag the images you want to use in the slideshow and then select Slideshow under the Tools menu.

The slideshow controls are in the lower right corner of the screen. You can hide or display the controls by pressing Ctrl-H or by right-clicking the mouse.

The control panel has buttons for Back, Forward, Play, Pause, and Stop. The Back and Forward buttons show the previous or next slide and pause the slideshow.

The slider and up/down arrows are used to speed up or slow down the slideshow. You can also enter a specific speed, in frames per second. At the faster rates, your computer may not be able to load the images fast enough to keep up with the desired frames per second. If you encounter this, wait for a couple of years and the computers will be a lot faster.

To set the following slideshow options, select Options under the Tools menu:

  • Frames per Minute

  • Random Order

  • Show File Name

  • Show Controls
