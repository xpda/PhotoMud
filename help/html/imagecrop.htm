Cropping the Image

Image Cropping is a two-step process. First, use Set Cropping Area under the Image menu or the Cropping Tool in the toolbar to specify the area of the image to be cropped.

To set the cropping area, drag the cropping box to the desired location. You can press the Shift key down while dragging to show the image area outside the box. You can also release the mouse button and drag the sides of the cropping box individually for precise positioning.

After the cropping box is in the desired location, press Enter or use the Crop under the Image menu to actually crop or trim the image.

Image Cropping is done under Photo Mud Editor.
