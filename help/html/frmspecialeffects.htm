Special Effects

The Special Effects feature of Photo Mud provides several special effects you use with your image.

  • Emboss -- Shows and embossed effect on the image.

  • Posterize -- Posterize limits the image to a small number of color levels.

  • Mosaic -- Mosaic makes the image look like it's made of giant pixels. You can specify the cell size.

  • Solarize -- Solarize mimics the accidental exposure of film to sunlight. It inverts the color intensities larger than the specified threshold to produce an artistic effect.

  • Intensity Detect -- Intensity detect sets each pixel color to 0 or 255, depending on whether it is above or below the specified threshold. This may be useful for artistic effects as well as edge detection.

  • Add Random Noise

The Original and Modified images can be zoomed in or out using the command buttons between the images. You can drag the mouse across an image to move or pan the images. It may be useful to zoom in on the image to get a better idea of the changes being made.

The Special Effects command is under the Image menu of Photo Mud Editor.
