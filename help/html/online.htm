Online Registration, Update, and Purchasing

You can register your copy of Photo Mud on the internet by selected Register Photo Mud Online from the Help menu. This will make sure you get update notices for Photo Mud.

If have the trial version of Photo Mud, you can purchase Photo Mud online by selecting Purchase Photo Mud Online from the Help menu. After your purchase you will be emailed the Activation Key that will unlock the trial version and allow you to use Photo Mud indefinitely. To unlock the trial version, select Enter Activation Key from the Help menu and enter activation key along with your email address.

From time to time, updates to Photo Mud are available. You can check to see if you have the latest version any time by selecting Online Update from the Help menu.
