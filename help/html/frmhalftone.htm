Converting to Halftone

Halftone images are used for printing presses, and may produce better results as copy machine originals. There are several different halftone methods available with Photo Mud:

  • Print -- Halftone designed for printing

  • View -- Halftone designed for viewing on a computer screen.

  • Rectangular - Halftone with rectangular patterns

  • Circular - Halftone with rectangular patterns

  • Elliptical - Halftone with rectangular patterns

  • Random - Halftone with rectangular patterns

  • Linear - Halftone with rectangular patterns

These methods may have selectable angles, and all but the Print and View methods have selectable pattern sizes.

The Original and Modified images can be zoomed in or out using the command buttons between the images. You can drag the mouse across an image to move or pan the images. It may be useful to zoom in on the image to get a better idea of the changes being made.

The Halftone command is under the Tools menu of Photo Mud Editor.
