Adding Text to Your Image

The Text command under the Draw menu of Photo Mud Editor is used to add text to an image. You can choose from a variety of fonts and 3D effects.

After selecting the font and effects, enter the text in the Sample Text field and click OK. The text will be shown on the image with a selection boundary. The text will follow the cursor on the screen. Move the text to the desired location and press Enter.

Be careful -- if you forget to press Enter to fix the text's location, the text will be lost.
