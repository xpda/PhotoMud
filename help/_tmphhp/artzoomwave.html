Artistic Effect: Zoom Wave
 Previous Top Next

The Zoom Wave effect transforms the photo with waves radiating out from a center point. You can specify the center point by clicking on the left (original) copy of the photo. You can use the right (modified) copy for panning and zooming.

You can specify the wave amplitude, number of waves, the radial angle, and the wave zoom factor. The wave zoom factor determines how "sharp" the waves are.
