Artistic Effect: Ripples
 Previous Top Next

The Ripples effect make a photo appear as if it were being reflected off water after a small stone was tossed in. The ripples expand out from any point in the photo. To set that point, click on the left (original) copy of the photo.

You can specify the wave height, frequency (how close they are together), attenuation (how fast the waves die out as they expand), and phase (the position of the waves relative to the center point.)

You can also specify the background color, which may appear near the edges of the photo.
