Brightness, Contrast, Saturation
 Previous Top Next

You can set the brightness, contrast, and saturation of a photo by sliding the bars with the mouse or by entering the amount of change. The Image brightness and contrast adjust all colors together. Alternatively, each color can be set independently using Color Adjustment under the Color menu.

You can zoom in and out on the original and modified photos with the + and - buttons and with the mouse wheel. It may be useful to zoom in on the image to get a better idea of the changes being made. You can pan or move the photos by dragging the mouse on either photo.

Brightness, Contrast, Saturation is in the Color menu of Photo Mud Editor.
