Special Effects
 Previous Top Next

The Special Effects feature of Photo Mud provides several common special effects you can apply to Photos. Click on any of the following for details and examples.

·    Emboss
·    Posterize
·    Mosaic
·    Solarize
·    Intensity Detect
·    Add Random Noise

You can zoom in and out on the original and modified photos with the + and - buttons and with the mouse wheel. It may be useful to zoom in on the image to get a better idea of the changes being made. You can pan or move the photos by dragging the mouse on either photo.

Special Effects is under the Image menu of Photo Mud Editor. Additional effects can be found in Artistic Effects.
