Color Adjustment
 Previous Top Next

Color Adjustment under the Color menu can be used to adjust the red, green, and blue intensities in a photo. To adjust the color, move the slider for that color or enter a value from -100 to 100.

You can adjust the color settings for the shadow, midtone, and highlights of a photo. The midtone setting can be used to affect the overall color, with little or no change in the shadows and highlights. This often preserves a realistic look in the bright and dark areas of the photo.
