Select Similar Colors  
Previous Top Next

You can select an area in the photo of similar color to a specified point using Select Area of Similar Color under the Edit menu, and clicking the mouse on color in the photo you want to select. That part of the photo with a color similar to the color you designated will become a selection.

The Color Tolerance is the range of color intensities to be included. The larger the number, the wider the selection in the image.

There is an upper and a lower tolerance. The upper tolerance specifies the range of colors to be selected that are brighter than the color at the designated pixel. The lower tolerance specifies the range of colors to be selected that are darker than the color at the designated pixel.

The designated pixel is the point in the photo that was clicked on.
