You can zoom in and out on the original and modified photos with the + and - buttons and with the mouse wheel. It may be useful to zoom in on the image to get a better idea of the changes being made. You can pan or move the photos by dragging the mouse on either photo.

Artistic Effects is under the Image menu of Photo Mud Editor.
