Filling an Area of Similar Color    
Previous Top Next

You can fill an area of similar color using Fill Area of Similar Color under the Draw menu or toolbar, and clicking the mouse on color in the photo you want to fill. That part of the photo with a color similar to the color you selected will be filled with the current drawing color.

The Color Tolerance is the range of color intensities to be included in the filled area. The larger the number, the wider the selection in the photo.

There is an upper and a lower tolerance. The upper tolerance specifies the range of colors to be selected that are brighter than the color at the designated pixel. The lower tolerance specifies the range of colors to be selected that are darker than the color at the designated pixel.

The designated pixel is the point in the photo that was clicked on.
