Printing a Calendar
 Previous Top Next

To print a calendar, first select or tag the photos you want to be printed in the Calendar. Then select Print Calendar from the Tools menu of Photo Mud Explorer. Normally 12 photos are used for a 12-month calendar, but you can select any number of photos and you can print a calendar for any number of months (up to 100 years).

Photos Tab

The first tab in the Calendar function is for photo selection and descriptions.

  

Enter the beginning month and year for the calendar, and the number of months to be printed. Then you can review the photo for each month, optionally adding a description.

If there are too few photos for the number of months in the calendar, the photos will be repeated until the calendar is complete. If there are more photos than months, then the surplus photos will not be printed.

You can change the order of the photos with the arrow keys. The month for a photo is shown underneath its image.

After the photos and months have been selected, click on Next or the Print Options tab.

Print Options Tab

The Print Options allow you to specify the calendar format, printing options, and special dates to appear in the calendar.

 

The standard printer options are in the upper left, included printer selection, printer preferences button, font, number of copies, and margin information.

The Calendar title will appear at the bottom of each month.

Holidays and other dates, such as moon phases and eclipses, can be listed on the calendar. Select any or all categories you would like to appear in the calendar.

The information categories you select will be displayed in the sample calendar on the screen. You can browse forward and backward through the months using the arrow keys at the lower right of the screen. You can also maximize the dialog, or drag a corner to make it larger so you can see the month better.

One of the best features of the Photo Mud Calendar is the ability to add your own special dates. You can add birthdays of family and friends, anniversaries of important events, and special events in the upcoming year.

To add dates for your own custom events, click on the Edit button at the top of the list of categories. You will be able to add new event categories or edit existing categories for the calendar. Photo Mud comes with the "Birthdays and Anniversaries" custom event category already configured.

  

You can move back and forth between the Photos and the Printing Options tabs to change settings. To do this, click on the top of the tab or on the Previous or Next button.

Printing Options

You can print a calendar with the photo and month on separate pages or on the same page. You can print all pages of the calendar, or you can print only odd or even pages to facilitate double- sided printing.

When you assemble a calendar, you can bind the pages together between the month and the photo with a spiral or comb binding, or something similar. If you prefer to print the month and the photo on the same page, you can print the calendar so it's easy to staple together. Here's how:

1.   Select "Print photo and calendar on the same page."
2.   Select "Print Odd Pages"
3.   Select "Order Pages for Stapling"
4.   Select the other options, maybe portrait orientation and centered.
5.   Click on the Print button.
6.   After the pages print, load them back into the printer so the new pages will print on the blank side of the paper, upside down.
7.   Select the "Print Even Pages" option and leave the other settings the way they were.
8.   Click on the Print button.
9.   You should now be able to staple the pages in the center to make a calendar.
